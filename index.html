About Me:

Hello World! My name is Robert William Ashley, but everyone calls me Will. I am a secondary year PhD Student in Learning, Design and Technology at Penn State. In 2015 I receieved my Bachlor degree in Psychology with a concentration in Counseling at Mississippi State University.

Avid Sailor: +

Most of my life I grew up in a small town on the coast of Mississippi. Growing up on the coast was amazing because it allowed me to take up sailing as a hobby. From kindergarten to senior year of high school I was an avid sailor. I became the captain of the sailing team at my high school, were we became State Champions in a Team Racing Regatta, as well as qualified for the Baker and Mallory championship.

Hurricane Katrina: +

One problem with growing up on the coast was dealing with hurricanes. In 2005, Hurricane Katrina made landfall on the Gulf Coast. My family had evactuated to Florida and we soon learned that our house was detroyed. Eleven years later you can still see the impact Hurricane Katrina had on the Gulf Coast, but for the most part we have rebuilt.
